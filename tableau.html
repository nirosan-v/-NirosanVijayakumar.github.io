    Tableau

    I have a strong command of Tableau's data visualisation and analytical capabilities. My expertise includes creating various types of graphs such as bar charts, line charts, scatter plots and density maps. I use calculated fields and bins for data manipulation and apply inner, outer, left, and right joins to merge datasets efficiently. Additionally, I build interactive dashboards that allow dynamic exploration of data and enable decision-makers to gain meaningful insights. These dashboards, combined with advanced tools like conditional formatting and data blending, allow me to turn large datasets into clear, actionable visualisations that support data-driven solutions. Tableau empowers me to translate complex datasets into user-friendly insights that drive business decisions.
